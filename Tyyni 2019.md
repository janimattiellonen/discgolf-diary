## Tyyni Disc setup

- Orange Teebird
- Green Teebird
- Black/Orange Teebird (own dye)

- Sidewinder

- TL (Pink)
- PA1
- 2 Harps
- 2 PA3
- 3 Mako3 (1 beaten pink, 1 newer pink, 1 blue)
- 2 Roc3 (both XXL stamped discs)
